Hi, I'm Thomas! I'm a first year PhD student working on mechanistic interpretability in machine learning.

My research focusses on understanding neural networks from their weights. This allows us to make guarantees about their behaviour and failure modes.

Machine learning models have become an undeniably useful tool in recent years. Yet, they remain shrouded in mystery; both in research and everyday use. Understanding these models will not only help their trustworthiness but guarantee their utility.

Outside of work, I love to go on long bike rides, game with friends, go skiing and wear shorts.
